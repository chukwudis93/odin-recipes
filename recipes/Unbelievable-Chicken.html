Unbelievable-Chicken Recipes

This Unbelievable-Chicken Recipe is a simple yet incredibly flavoured dish that proves you don't need complicated steps to make amazing food. Tender chicken pieces are soaked in rich, savory marinde made from evertyday that blend sweet, tangy, and slightly spicy flavors. As the chicken cooks, the marinde seeps deep into the meat, creating a juicy texture and a mouth-watering aroma that immediatly grabs attention.

What makes this recipe truly "unbelievable" is how effortlessly it delivers restaurant-quality taste at home. whether baked, grilled, or pan-fried, the chicken comes out golden on the outside and perfectly moist inside. it's the kind of recipe that surprises everyone at the table-simple to prepare, yet unfogettable with every bite.

Ingredient

  • Chicken pieces(breast or thighs)
  • Vegetable oil or olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Sugar or honey
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Wash and clean the chiken pieces thoroughly
  2. mix soy sauce, ketchup, oil, sugar, garlic, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Add the chicken to the mixture and coat it well.
  4. Cover and marinate for at least 30minutes.
  5. Cook the chicken by baking, grillling, or pan-frying until fully done
  6. Serve hot enjoy.
